About Us

Our Story

It all began in 2007 with a simple idea to create spaces that don’t just look good, but work better. Today, Intech Infra Solutions is a full-service, award-winning turnkey interior design company trusted by businesses across India. We specialise in commercial projects, working with properties between 10,000 and 2,00,000 sq. ft., with most of our work centred around 50,000 to 75,000 sq. ft.

As a single-source solution provider, we handle everything from design and contracting to installation and premium furniture- ensuring every detail is aligned from start to finish. Our projects span IT parks, BPOs, co-working spaces, educational institutions, and warehouses, each one shaped with care, functionality, and future-ready thinking.

At the core of everything we do is a team that believes in clear process, bold ideas, and interiors that quietly deliver on both.

Our Mission

To transform everyday workspaces into extraordinary environments through innovative, efficient, and aesthetically-driven turnkey interior design solutions. We strive to uplift work experiences by delivering modular, ergonomic, and scalable interiors that reflect both brand ethos and employee wellbeing.

Our Vision

To grow from a ₹30 crore enterprise to a ₹100 crore powerhouse within the next year by scaling our operations, expanding our footprint across India, and setting new benchmarks in turnkey commercial interiors and engineering consultancy services. Our goal is to become a globally recognised design studio known for timeless spaces and transformative experiences.

One Team, Many Minds

At Intech Infra, every space we craft is a symphony of expertise, imagination, and collaboration. Our team—engineers sketching blueprints, interior designers weaving stories through textures, visual artists painting possibilities, and project managers orchestrating it all—brings diverse talents to a shared vision.

We thrive on the spark of ideas exchanged over coffee, the challenge of solving complex problems, and the joy of seeing a concept transform into reality. Cross-functional thinking fuels our innovation, continuous learning sharpens our craft, and a relentless pursuit of excellence drives every nail, every finish, every detail.

Together, we don’t just build spaces—we create environments that inspire, endure, and exceed expectations.
